Pre-story

B

It was a huge project for the planet earth. Humanity's first attempt to actively reach out to outer-space

: A 10year-mission to connect with other life forms and return to earth. Many Astronauts were involved in this project and on this spaceship, our spaceman X was born.

However, as I said, our story started after this one ended. Every astronaut was killed by an unfortunate incident leaving young X all alone.
At that moment, our story begins.

Daily Routine

B

Living alone, X doesn't have much diversity in his everyday life. Still, he has a daily routine to keep himself busy.

First, washing up is important. It prevents him from catching a cold. Also, X quite enjoy the soap bubbles.

Next, X eats food. The ship has a container to grow some fruits and vegetables. There is a huge amount of dried pasta that he can cook but it won't last forever.

When he has finished the necessary step, X usually spends time to observe Earth. He dreams about the people and the "normal" life on the planet Earth. He calculates ways back to Earth with his computer but it not always successful.

After he has done working, X watches old movies and play solitaire. X loves to imitate the actors on screen. He memorizes all the words perfectly. Solitaire may be a tedious game, but there are some shuffles that X struggles with.

X usually fall asleep in front of the tv while watching a movie so he could dream about Earth.
